Maui, Hawaii

A tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, lush landscapes, and vibrant culture.

Top 5 Attractions:

  1. Haleakalā National Park: Witness the breathtaking sunrise from the summit of this dormant volcano.
  2. Road to Hana: Explore the scenic drive along the winding road, passing waterfalls, lush rainforests, and scenic viewpoints.
  3. Wailea Beach: Relax on the pristine white sands and enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing.
  4. Lahaina Town: Discover the historic whaling village with charming shops, galleries, and restaurants.
  5. Molokini Crater: Snorkel in the crystal-clear waters of this volcanic crater, home to a diverse marine ecosystem.

Must-Try Foods:

  • Poke: Sample the fresh and flavorful Hawaiian-style raw fish salad.
  • Shave Ice: Cool off with this refreshing treat made with finely shaved ice and flavored syrups.
  • Kalua Pork: Indulge in the tender, slow-cooked pork, traditionally cooked in an underground imu oven.
  • Lū‘au Feast: Experience the traditional Hawaiian feast featuring a variety of local dishes like poi, laulau, and haupia.
  • Malasadas: Treat yourself to these delicious Portuguese-style fried doughnuts, often filled with flavored cream or custard.
